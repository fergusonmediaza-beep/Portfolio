About Queens Way Capital

Queens Way Capital is built by seasoned technology operators, strategic advisors, and investment professionals with decades of experience across the African tech ecosystem. Our team understands the realities of building, scaling, and sustaining technology businesses in emerging markets.

We don’t fund or finance — we guide. Using our proprietary assessment models, we analyse organisational readiness, leadership capability, product viability, and market opportunity. This gives our clients a clear, grounded view of where they stand and the strategic direction needed to move forward.
